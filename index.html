SearchOCR Privacy Policy

At SearchOCR, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Collection and Use of Personal Information

SearchOCR does not collect any personal information about you or your device. We do not require you to provide any personal information to use our app.

Processing of Images and Files

SearchOCR performs Optical Character Recognition (OCR) processing on images and files that you choose to open with our app. All OCR processing is done locally on your Mac and no images or files are sent to our servers or any third-party servers for processing.

Data Retention

SearchOCR does not retain any images or files processed through our app. Once the OCR processing is completed, the results are displayed to you within our app. We do not store or share any of the content of the processed images or files.

Security Measures

SearchOCR takes appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your data.
